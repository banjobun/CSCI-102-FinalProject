Events

 

Come Check us out at the Farmer's Market!

Pete's Pets will have a booth set up at the local market. Come check out our outdoor supplies and help us socialize our puppies by playing with them.

 

Elementary School Visits

If you are intersted in having Pete's Pets come to your school, visit our Contact page to send us your information. We offer fun and educational visits, plus visited students will recieve an in-store discount.

 

Fundraisers

Pete's Pets hold a monthly fundraiser to benefit local animal shelters and local food banks. If you wish to contribute, please visit our Contact page.
